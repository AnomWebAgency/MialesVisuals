We Live For Passion

Hello, my name is Miales, and welcome you to Miales visuals. I'm a wedding and lifestyle photographer based in Harare Zimbabwe. What started as a hobby soon became a career fueled with a fiery passion for capturing the authentic emotions of couples and all the beautiful details that make up their wedding day.

Within 8 years as a luxury wedding and lifestyle photographer, mialesvisuals have shot the cover of two major bridal magazines: Munaluchi Bride and Zim Bridal Expo 3rd edition, and our works featured in Wakanaka, Divas Inc, The Champ, Forbes Africa Under 30, and more.
